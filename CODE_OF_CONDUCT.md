# Code Of Conduct

This project adopts the Contributor Covenant 2.1.

## Our Pledge

We want PSON5 to be a professional, technically rigorous, and welcoming open-source project. Contributors, maintainers, and community members are expected to:

- act respectfully and in good faith
- debate ideas directly without personal attacks
- prioritize clarity, evidence, and reproducibility
- avoid harassment, discrimination, intimidation, or abuse

## Expected Behavior

- Give actionable technical feedback.
- Assume good faith until evidence shows otherwise.
- Keep discussions on-topic and constructive.
- Respect privacy and security concerns, especially around user data.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or demeaning language
- Publication of private or sensitive information without permission
- Bad-faith technical sabotage or intentionally misleading guidance
- Disruptive behavior that prevents others from contributing

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or suspend participation for behavior that violates this code of conduct.

## Reporting

Report issues privately through the security/support channels in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) and [SUPPORT.md](SUPPORT.md).
